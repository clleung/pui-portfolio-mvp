re:Bloom Consulting Project: Royally Fit Website Redesign and Redevelopment

Pittsburgh, PA


Timeline: April - June 2021.

Team: Abbey Mui (Project Manager), Ademitunde Adenuga

Role: Volunteer Designer and Developer

Tools: Shopify, PageSpeed Insights, Figma, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, jQuery, Zoom.

Skills: Front-End Programming, UI/UX Design, SEO, User Testing & Interviews.

Deliverables: Marketing Strategy, Brand Kit, Surveys, SEO Keywords, Prototype, Redeveloped Website.


Overview

re:Bloom is a nonprofit geared towards improving the online presence of small businesses in Pittsburgh. For our project, we worked with Royally Fit, an organic skincare and products.

Before we began the project, the client’s website had a very low conversion rate. The website had loading issues and did not clearly communicate the mission of the business—namely, the fact that all of the profits went towards community wellness events.

To improve the conversion rate, we used PageSpeed Insights to find areas that were slowing down the website and conducted market research to determine pain points for potential customers. From the data we collected, we decided to tackle the issue from multiple directions: site restructuring to improve navigation and remove inefficiently used code or files; a new brand kit, to refresh the look of the website and improve readability; best practices for future optimization and marketing for the client.
